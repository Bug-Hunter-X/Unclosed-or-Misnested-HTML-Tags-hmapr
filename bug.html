This is a common HTML error, but it can be easily missed. The issue is caused by a missing closing tag or an incorrect nesting of tags. It often occurs when working with nested elements like divs, spans, or tables. Browsers try to correct these errors, but the behavior can be unpredictable and lead to unexpected layout and styling issues.

Example:

<div>
  <p>This is a paragraph.<p>
</div>

In this example, the <p> tag is missing its closing tag </p>. This will lead to unexpected layout issues.
